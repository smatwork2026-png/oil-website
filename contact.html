FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our products and services.

How long does delivery take?

We deliver within 2-3 business days in major cities. Remote areas may take 4-5 business days. Free delivery on orders above Rs. 3,000.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 7-day return policy for unopened products. If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, we'll replace it free of charge.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Cash on Delivery (COD), bank transfer, and online payment via JazzCash and EasyPaisa. WhatsApp orders are also available.

Is your olive oil authentic?

Absolutely! Our olive oil is 100% pure, cold-pressed, and organic. We provide quality certificates upon request to ensure authenticity.
