History

The Ancient Gift of Olive Oil

Olive oil has been treasured for thousands of years, valued for its health benefits, culinary uses, and cultural significance.

Ancient Origins

Olive oil production dates back to 4000 BCE in the Mediterranean region. Ancient civilizations considered it "liquid gold" and used it for food, medicine, and rituals.

Cultural Significance

Throughout history, olive oil has held sacred importance in many religions and cultures. The olive branch symbolizes peace, wisdom, and prosperity.

Medicinal Uses

Ancient physicians like Hippocrates documented over 60 medicinal uses for olive oil. Modern science continues to validate these traditional health claims.

Global Legacy

Today, olive oil is celebrated worldwide as one of the healthiest cooking oils, a key component of the Mediterranean diet recognized by UNESCO.

Our Process

Cold Press Extraction

Our olive oil is extracted using traditional cold press methods to preserve maximum flavor and nutrients.

1. Handpicking

Olives are carefully handpicked at the perfect stage of ripeness to ensure optimal flavor and nutritional content.

2. Washing

Fresh olives are thoroughly cleaned using pure water to remove any leaves, twigs, or debris before processing.

3. Crushing

Olives are crushed into a paste using traditional stone mills, preserving the delicate flavors and aromas.

4. Cold Pressing

The paste is pressed at temperatures below 27Â°C to extract oil without damaging its nutrients or flavor.
